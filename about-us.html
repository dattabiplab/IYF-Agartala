About ISKCON Youth Forum Agartala

The ISKCON Youth Forum (IYF) Agartala is a dedicated wing of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON) that focuses on nurturing and guiding the youth toward a life of purpose, devotion, and joy. Situated in Math Chowmuhani, Agartala, the forum offers a welcoming space where young minds can explore spiritual wisdom, develop leadership skills, and engage in uplifting community activities. Our mission is to help youth harmonize their personal, academic, and professional lives with timeless spiritual values.

Srila Prabhupada – The Visionary Founder

His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Srila Prabhupada, the founder-acharya of ISKCON, is the guiding inspiration for our forum. In 1965, Srila Prabhupada traveled to the West at the age of 69 with the aim of sharing the ancient wisdom of the Bhagavad Gita and Srimad Bhagavatam with the world. Through tireless effort, he established ISKCON in 1966 in New York City and inspired millions globally.
Srila Prabhupada envisioned youth as torchbearers of a spiritual revolution—leaders who could blend modern education with Vedic wisdom to create a compassionate and God-conscious society. IYF Agartala strives to carry forward this vision by providing mentorship, education, and engagement opportunities that help young people realize their highest potential.

Courses – Learning with Purpose

Our forum offers various spiritual and personality development courses tailored for students, professionals, and seekers. Each course combines philosophical knowledge with practical application, helping participants lead balanced and happy lives.
Some of our popular courses include:

  • Bhagavad Gita – The Art of Living: A verse-by-verse exploration of the Gita’s wisdom.
  • Īśopaniṣad – The Vision of the Supreme: Understanding the 18 mantras of divine knowledge.
  • Discover Yourself: A short course introducing key spiritual concepts for self-realization.
  • Japa & Meditation Workshops: Hands-on training for mantra meditation.
  • Devotee Etiquette & Lifestyle: Learning the values and culture of devotional living.
  • Spiritual Leadership Program: Designed to help youth lead projects and teams with compassion.

    • These courses are conducted in a friendly, interactive format with discussions, presentations, and Q&A sessions to ensure participants gain both understanding and inspiration.

    Events – Connecting People with Devotion

  • Workshops & Seminars: Covering topics like stress management, time management, and personal transformation.
  • Outreach Programs in Colleges & Schools: Bringing spiritual knowledge directly to students.

    Mentors – Guiding with Care

    Our mentors are experienced devotees who provide intellectual and spiritual guidance to youth members. They act as friends, counselors, and role models, helping individuals navigate life’s challenges with wisdom and clarity.
    Mentorship involves:

  • One-on-one discussions
  • Group guidance sessions

    • The mentor-mentee relationship at IYF Agartala is built on trust, mutual respect, and a shared commitment to growth.

    Festivals – Celebrating Devotion

    Festivals are the heartbeat of IYF. We celebrate major Vaishnava and cultural festivals with grandeur, joy, and devotion:

  • Janmashtami – The appearance day of Lord Krishna with midnight arati, kirtan, and cultural performances.
  • Ratha Yatra – The grand chariot festival of Lord Jagannath.
  • Jhulan Yatra – Swing festival of Radha and Krishna.
  • Gaura Purnima – Celebrating the appearance of Lord Chaitanya Mahaprabhu.
  • Nityananda Trayodashi – The appearance day of Lord Nityananda.

    • Youth actively participate in all aspects—decorations, kirtan, drama, prasadam, and crowd management—making festivals a community-driven celebration.

    Divya Darshanam – Beholding the Lord

    One of the most uplifting aspects of IYF Agartala is the opportunity for Divya Darshanam—the divine vision of the Lord in His beautiful deity form.
    Devotees can offer prayers, participate in aratis, and meditate upon the Lord’s enchanting form. The serene atmosphere of the temple, coupled with devotional music and incense fragrance, provides a deeply transformative experience.

    Location – In the Heart of Math Chowmuhani

    Our center is located in Math Chowmuhani, Agartala, making it easily accessible for students and working professionals.
    The location is central yet peaceful, providing an ideal environment for spiritual gatherings. Its proximity to educational institutions and residential areas makes it a convenient hub for youth activities.

    Conclusion

    ISKCON Youth Forum Agartala is more than just a group—it’s a family of like-minded young individuals united by a desire to live meaningfully and joyfully.

    Whether through studying Srila Prabhupada’s books, participating in vibrant festivals, going on spiritual trips, or simply singing together in kirtan, every moment at IYF Agartala is an opportunity to grow—in devotion, in knowledge, and in happiness.
