Our Mentors

HG Abhiram Nimai Das

Youth Counselor

Committed youth counselor and key manager for ISKCON Agartala, he actively conducts workshops at top professional institutions such as medical and engineering colleges. His outreach includes meetings with the governor and other high-ranking officials, promoting Krishna consciousness and strengthening ISKCON’s presence across influential circles.

HG Sridham Govind Das

Senior Mentor

With over 40 years in ISKCON, holding key managerial roles such as RSA Western Odisha, IBAC member, General Secretary of India Tribal Care Trust, and Chairman of Srikhetra Parikrama, actively leading outreach initiatives for India’s tribal communities and contributing extensively to ISKCON’s organizational and spiritual growth.

HG Krishna Radhakanta Das

Bhakti Yoga Instructor

One of the top preachers in the Northeastern Indian region, he actively serves in Tribal Care Trust, Food for Life, and free medical camps. Renowned for delivering various courses on Bhakti-yoga, he inspires countless individuals to embrace Krishna consciousness through his dedicated service and compassionate outreach.

HG Suhrt Sudamavipra Das

Men's Forum Head

Principal of Bharat Pharmaceutical Technology, Agartala & a PhD holder. He is widely regarded as one of the most positive and inspiring figures in the community. As Head of Men’s Forum, he leads with wisdom and dedication, guiding men and youth towards a stronger Krishna conscious life.

HG Purnesvari Radhika Devi Dasi

Women's Forum Head

The Women’s Forum Lead of ISKCON Agartala and a respected government professional, regarded as a highly influential figure in the community. Known for inspiring and empowering women through spiritual guidance, leadership, and active service, fostering growth in Krishna consciousness among devotees and the wider society.

Bhakta Titan Saha

Youth Co-ordinator

Dedicated teacher at DWP School and a Master’s degree holder in Physics, is committed to guiding and inspiring youth. Actively serving in ISKCON Youth Forum, he blends academic excellence with spiritual values to nurture discipline, devotion, and character among the younger generation.
